# Analysis

Hercules Capital, Inc. (formerly Hercules Technology Growth Capital) is one of the largest business development companies focused on private credit and venture lending. At current prices, the company presents an attractive opportunity for yield-focused investors.

## Investment Thesis

HTGC has built a dominant market position in venture lending, a nascent but rapidly growing asset class. The company's advantages:

- **Scale & LPs:** $11B+ in AUM with institutional LP base
- **Underwriting:** Discipline on pricing and structure
- **Portfolio:** Diversified across stages and sectors
- **NAV momentum:** Consistent NAV accretion

## Financial Overview

Based on most recent pricing (March 31, 2026):

**Per Share Metrics**
- NAV: $22.15
- Dividend Yield: 8.2%
- Quarterly Distribution: $0.40

**Portfolio Composition**
- Venture Debt: 68%
- Direct Equity: 22%
- Other: 10%

## Valuation Framework

We employ a dividend discount model given HTGC's defined payout policy:

**Base Case Assumptions**
- Annual dividend: $1.60 (current run rate)
- 3% long-term growth
- 8.5% required return

This yields a fair value of $21.00 per share, suggesting 8% upside from $19.42.

**Bear Case ($14.50)**
- Dividend cut to $1.28 due to portfolio stress
- 10% required return (higher risk premium)
- 1% growth

**Bull Case ($27.00)**
- Dividend grows to $1.92 on portfolio expansion
- 7.5% required return (lower risk as strategy de-risks)
- 4% growth

## Key Risks

1. **Credit cycle risk:** Venture environment could deteriorate, impairing portfolio companies
2. **Rate sensitivity:** Rising rates reduce discount factors but improve lending spreads
3. **Scale risk:** Rapid AUM growth could create operational challenges
4. **Regulatory change:** Changes to BDC rules could impact economics

## Conclusion

At $19.42, Hercules Capital offers an attractive risk-adjusted entry point for the yield-oriented investor comfortable with private credit exposure.
